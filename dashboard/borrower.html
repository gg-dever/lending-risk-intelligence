Borrower Profile Risk Analysis

Beyond grade and term, borrower characteristics including debt to income ratio and home ownership provide secondary risk signals. Higher DTI constrains repayment capacity; renters show elevated default rates relative to homeowners.

Debt to Income (DTI) Analysis

DTI ratio measures monthly debt burden relative to income. Higher DTI constrains repayment capacity.

Home Ownership

Home ownership indicates asset accumulation and suggests skin in the game for borrowers.

Borrower profile dimensions are secondary risk signals. Grade and term remain the primary drivers.
