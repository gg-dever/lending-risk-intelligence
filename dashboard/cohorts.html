Crisis Era Impact

Crisis era loans (2008 to 2010) may show atypical default behavior in this dataset due to reporting and composition differences versus later vintages. This section is provided as a stress period reference rather than a baseline estimate. Potential survivorship effects and changes in data capture may understate realized losses for this period, so comparisons with post-2011 cohorts should be interpreted with caution.
