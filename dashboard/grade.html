Risk Analysis by Loan Grade

Loan grade is the primary risk dimension. Default rates escalate monotonically from 5.8% in grade A to 49.3% in grade G. Grades A and B account for 44% of portfolio count but only 18% of expected losses. Grades C and D together drive 55% of losses despite representing 43% of portfolio value, the core concentration risk of this book.
