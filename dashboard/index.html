Portfolio Summary

The lending portfolio is approximately 18.5 billion dollars across about 1.27 million loans. The portfolio-wide default rate is approximately 19.6 percent, with risk concentrated in grade B and C loans, which represent 55 percent of portfolio value and about 48 percent of expected losses. Expected loss is estimated at roughly 1.1 billion dollars, equivalent to a 5.9 percent loss rate under a 70 percent recovery assumption on defaulted loans. Overall risk appears moderate to elevated, supported by concentration in 60 month loans, which account for 56 percent of expected losses while representing 21 percent of loan count.
